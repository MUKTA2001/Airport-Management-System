Privacy Notice

Your privacy is important to us. This notice explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our Airport Management System.

What Information We Collect

  • Your name, contact details, and passport information
  • Flight bookings and travel details
  • Payment and transaction details
  • Device information, IP address, and website activity
  • Security-related details such as identity verification

How We Use Your Information

  • To manage your flight bookings and airport services
  • To provide customer support and resolve issues
  • To enhance security, prevent fraud, and comply with legal obligations
  • To improve our services and website performance
  • To send important updates, alerts, or marketing emails (if you opt in)

How We Keep Your Data Safe

We use the latest security measures, including:

  • SSL encryption for secure data transmission
  • Regular system monitoring for security threats
  • Access controls and multi-factor authentication
  • Data storage in secured servers

Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and tracking technologies to:

  • Enhance user experience and personalize content
  • Analyze website traffic and improve services
  • Ensure security and prevent unauthorized access

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal data
  • Request data deletion or restriction
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • File a complaint if you believe your data is misused

Third-Party Services

We may share limited data with trusted third-party partners for:

  • Payment processing and fraud prevention
  • Security verification and identity checks
  • Website analytics and performance monitoring

We do not sell your personal data to any third parties.

Data Retention

We keep your data only as long as necessary for the purposes stated in this notice or as required by law. After that, we securely delete or anonymize it.

Legal Compliance

We comply with international data protection laws and cooperate with authorities to ensure your rights are protected.
